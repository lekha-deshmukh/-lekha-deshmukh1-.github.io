Creating AI prompts for Google Ads Landing Pages used by 100+ Premium Clients by building a scalable framework for Landing Pages

Objective

Design a scalable high-conversion landing page framework that generates highly qualified leads for clients across 20+ domains using AI-powered Google Ads.

Key Achievements

  • 130+ impactful landing pages delivered to over 100 clients.
  • 25,000+ high-quality leads generated.
  • Reduced landing page creation time from 25 days to 20 minutes over 1.5 years.
  • Helped 100+ Clients across domains like Software services, manufacturing, consulting, education, interior design, home decoration, financial services, Elevators, tours and travels, waterproofing, Real estate, NA Plots, solar, automobile, NGO, will making, environment, lockers, Healthcare, Jewellery.

Context

To kickstart any successful Google Ads campaign, an optimized and high-performing landing page is critical. This case study showcases the development and refinement of a landing page framework that enhances efficiency, streamlines processes, and ensures timely delivery of high-quality leads.

More on context - The Market Gap Addressed by Inaiways

The Inaiways AI-powered Google Ads platform bridges a significant market gap by dynamically generating contextual landing pages, ad content & ad setup. This system optimizes ads continuously, 24/7, using real-time user search patterns to maximize relevance and conversions.

Business Concerns

  • Increasing number of customers for next funding round.
  • Landing page + ad setup took 45 days; goal was to bring delivery under 10 days.
  • Lack of website content led to delays; goal was to overcome this bottleneck.

My Role

As Co-Founder and UX Lead, I:

  • Spearheaded the landing page creation process from research to execution.
  • Trained and mentored the team to adopt Lean UX principles.
  • Collaborated with clients, stakeholders, and cross-functional teams to align with business KPIs.

Business KPIs for the UX Department

  • Build the team's capacity to serve 30+ clients/month.
  • Reduce landing page delivery timelines to under 10 days.

Logging and Communication

A structured Google Drive folder and Excel tracker were created for organizing assets and tracking milestones. Omnifocus prioritized tasks. Slack channels and regular huddles kept the team aligned, while feedback was collected using Playtailwind links and documented in Excel for better iteration.

Lean UX Process: Think, Make, Check
Iteration 1: Building the Foundation - Jan to March 2023
Think
  • Objective: Develop a repeatable and scalable framework to reduce delivery time.
  • Research & Insights:
    • Studied Sabri Suby’s Sell Like Crazy book.
    • Learned CRO from Jim Huffman’s LinkedIn course.
    • Gained insights into user-intent design from Alisha Conlin-Hurd.
    • Learned emotionally resonant copywriting from Alex Cattoni.
    • Conducted stakeholder interviews for each domain to build patterns.
Make
  • Created 300+ reusable HTML templates for sections.
  • Built layouts for industries like manufacturing, healthcare, education, etc.
  • Trained team for Google Page Speed, competitive analysis, and MS Clarity use.
  • Adopted Sabri Suby’s framework, then iteratively improved from user behavior metrics.

Check
  • Used MS Clarity + paid usability testing to improve designs every 16 days.

Iteration 2: Process Optimization - Oct 2023 to Dec 2023
Think
  • Objective: Address bottlenecks from the first iteration and improve mobile-first designs.
  • Research Insights:
    • MS Clarity data showed that long scroll lengths reduced mobile engagement.
    • Interior design clients needed image-heavy layouts; manufacturing preferred concise sections.
    • Optional sections delayed delivery; goal was to defer them post-launch.
Make
  • Deferred optional sections (like "Why Choose Us", comparison tables) for post-launch updates.
  • Created service sections to group low-budget client offerings.
  • Introduced horizontal scrolling for image-heavy sections (e.g., interior design domains).
  • Removed top navigation for most domains except healthcare and education where it boosted conversions.
  • Merged problem and solution sections for manufacturing clients.
  • Expanded the team: 2 junior designers, 1 senior designer.
  • Trained team on color and font selection aligned with client branding.
Check
  • UX Results:
    • Reduced landing page creation time from 10 days to 3 days.
    • Mobile-first designs significantly improved for different industries.
  • Challenges:
    • Addressing client UI change requests while educating them with UX reports.
    • Documenting domain-specific landing page sequences for consistency.
    • Resolving issues of repetitive templates by promoting creative layouts among designers.
Iteration 3: AI-Powered Landing Pages - Starting June 2024
Think
  • Objective: Leverage AI for exponential scalability and reduce repetitive tasks.
  • Research Insights:
    • Manual workflows, even when optimized, were limiting scalability.
    • Patterns across domains like real estate, healthcare, and software could be standardized for AI prompts.
    • AI tools could automate repetitive tasks like layout generation and text formatting.
Make
  • Developed AI prompts for each landing page section with standardized input formats (e.g., headline character limits, description rules).
  • Collaborated with CEO to define prompt data formats ensuring consistency in branding (colors, fonts).
  • Created 15 domain-specific templates using team expertise.
  • Introduced a design system for consistency across all AI-generated pages.
Check
  • Results:
    • Landing page creation reduced to 20 minutes per page!
    • Scalability improved to 99.54%.
  • Challenges:
    • Educating channel partners and marketers about AI's capabilities and limits.
    • Initial AI outputs needed manual fine-tuning for better visual and branding consistency.
    • Training the internal team on AI prompt creation was time-intensive but critical for long-term scalability.

Final Impact Across All Iterations
  • Client Success Stories:
    • vGoldenage: Planning to set up a new old-age care facility due to 125% yearly revenue increase.
    • Will Jini: Expanded to international business; leads increased by 266%.
    • Fonzel: Acquired premium clients like Mercedes Benz, Tata Communications; planning next funding round.
  • Delivered 130+ landing pages across 20+ industries (software services, healthcare, education, financial services, manufacturing, etc.).
  • Reduced delivery timelines from 25 days to 10 days, then to 3 days, and now 20 minutes (with AI prompts).
  • Created a self-sustaining scalable framework for future campaign expansions.
Learnings Across Iterations
  • Client-Centric Adjustments: Early communication streamlined asset collection and reduced project delays.
  • Domain-Specific Patterns: Documenting domain-specific landing page sequences accelerated production and improved effectiveness.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Using MS Clarity and usability tests led to continuous, evidence-based improvements.
  • Team Efficiency:
    • Training on UI/UX, color theory, and layout principles empowered team members.
    • Well-documented onboarding processes made scaling easier.
  • AI Integration: Manual performance patterns were successfully translated into AI prompts for faster scalability while maintaining quality standards.
