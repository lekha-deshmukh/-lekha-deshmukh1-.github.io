About
Creating AI prompts for Google Ads Landing Pages used by 100+ Premium Clients by building a scalable framework for Landing Pages
Objective
Design a scalable high-conversion landing page framework that generates highly qualified leads for clients across 20+ domains using AI-powered Google Ads.
Key Achievements
  • 130+ impactful landing pages delivered to over 100 clients.
  • 25,000+ high-quality leads generated.
  • Reduced landing page creation time from 25 days to 20 minutes in 3 iterations over 1.5 years.
  • Helped 100+ Clients across different domains like - Software services, manufacturing, consulting, education(online/ offline) , interior design, home decoration, financial services, Elevators, tours and travels, waterproofing, Real estate, NA Plots, solar, automobile, NGO, will making, environment, lockers, Healthcare, Jewellery
Context
To kickstart any successful Google Ads campaign, an optimized and high-performing landing page is critical. This case study showcases the development and refinement of a landing page framework that I have designed to enhance efficiency, streamline processes, and ensure timely delivery for creation of landing pages for Google Ads that deliver high quality leads.
More on context - The Market Gap Addressed by Inaiways
The Inaiways AI-powered Google Ads platform bridges a significant market gap by dynamically generating contextual landing pages, ad content & ad set up. This system optimizes ads continuously, 24/7, using real-time user search patterns to maximize relevance and conversions.
Business Concerns
  • Increasing number of customers for next funding round
  • The landing page delivery along with ad set up took 45 days. Landing page creation time alone was 20-25 days. In order to serve more customers, goal was to bring the time to delivery to under 10 days.
  • At times when clients did not have a website we didn't get enough content at time. This led to delayed delivery more than decided timeline. Goal was to overcome this bottleneck.
My Role

  • Collaborated with clients, stakeholders, and cross-functional teams to align goals with business KPIs.

Iteration 2: Process Optimization - Oct 2023 to Dec 2023
Think
  • Objective: Address bottlenecks from the first iteration and improve mobile-first designs.
  • Research Insights:
    • MS Clarity data: Long scroll lengths reduced engagement on mobile devices, encouraging users to scroll faster and missing out on important sections.
    • Interior design clients required image-heavy layouts, manufacturing clients preferred concise, solution-focused sections.
    • Optional sections (e.g., comparison tables) data took time to be delivered by client often caused delays in project timelines.
Make
  • For Resolving Business Problems
  • Deferred optional sections like "Why Choose Us" for post-launch updates - We (Google ads team + Business team + Design Team + Client consent) decided to drop sections that needed extra time from clients, and add it at a later time - e.g. comparison table, why choose us section or even stats - this allowed us faster delivery and gave more time for discovering keywords for Google Ads..
  • For clients that did not have sufficient funds - we (Google ads team + Business team + Design Team + Client consent) decided to show all services as a new section and sell the premium service as the main offering, this allowed attention for all products in low budget.
  • Design:
  • Introduced horizontal scrolling for image-heavy sections in domains like interior design. Added horizontal scroll for problems and solutions section for interior domains to reduce mobile scroll, after qualitative inputs we found that for interior domains people are not interested in reading text they only want to see the images of the work interior designers have done.
  • Understood that top navigation is not needed in landing pages for all domains except healthcare and education domain. The personas searched only via top menus and it let to more conversions by 32% (education domain). In healthcare domain the qualitative analysis showed that personas identified landing pages with more menus as informative and more skilled to do the job compared to less menu or no top menu.
  • Merged problem and solution sections for manufacturing clients to streamline the experience.
  • Expanded the team by hiring:
    • Two junior designers for HTML/CSS support.
    • One senior designer to mediate workflows and ensure quality.
  • Conducted training sessions on:
    • Selecting colors and fonts inspired by client branding.
    • Trained new team in creation of landing pages in in-house editor.
Check
  • UX Results:
    • Reduced landing page creation time to 3 days.
    • Enhanced mobile-first designs tailored to domain-specific needs.
  • Challenges:
    • Business: Listen to the clients wishes as well if they want UI changes - if it works great! If it doesnt then show them UX report on why the changes are not working and make the necessary ui changes in landing page again.
    • Design: Discovered landing page patterns for each of our 20+ client domains. These patterns were observed after qualitative + quantitative feedback for the domains. I created a documentation for each domain - section wise details that should be shown in sequence. In case lead conversion is low - We ( Design team ) study quantitative feedback with MS clarity for client in case lead conversion is affected, unless new domain is in the picture in which case we talk to client customers as well.
    • Repetitive designs in templates required rethinking layout creativity - For resolving issue related to repetitive design - Took sessions with team on how to utilise different ui design for showcasing information, Motivated team to explore designs, shared team members with appreciation emails from .
Iteration 3: AI-Powered Landing Pages - Starting June 2024
Think
  • Objective: Leverage AI for exponential scalability and reduce repetitive tasks.
  • Research Insights:
    • Manual workflows, even with optimizations, were limiting scalability from investment point of view for our business.
    • Patterns identified across domains (e.g., real estate, software, and healthcare) could be standardized for AI.
    • AI tools offered opportunities to automate repetitive tasks like layout generation and text formatting. My role is to pour my knowledge of landing pages as an AI prompt for each section of the landing page for each domain.
Make
  • Developed AI prompts for each landing page section, standardizing inputs like:
    • Character limits for headlines and descriptions.
    • Collaborated with the CEO to define data format for AI prompt for design consistency (e.g., colors, fonts) & scaling up.
  • Created total 15 domain-specific templates with help of Design team.
  • Introduced a design system for branding consistency across all pages in AI Prompt.
Check
  • Results:
    • Reduced landing page creation time to 20 minutes per page!
    • Improved scalability to 99.54% from 3 days delivery time to 20 minutes per landing page.
  • Challenges:
    • Business: Educating channel partners/ marketers on the capabilities and limitations of AI tools.
    • Design: AI outputs required initial manual adjustments for visual appeal.
    • Team: Training team members on AI Prompt was time-intensive but necessary.

Learnings Across Iterations
  • Client-Centric Adjustments:
    • Proactively communicating with clients during the sales closure helped streamline asset collection and reduced delays in delivery timelines.
    • Flexibility in addressing UI change requests ensured client satisfaction while maintaining design standards.
  • Domain-Specific Patterns: Recognizing emerging patterns across industries allowed us to document tailored landing page sequences for 20+ domains, making future projects faster and more effective.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: User behavior insights from tools like MS Clarity enabled iterative improvements.
  • Team Efficiency:
    • Training team members on UI/UX principles, color selection, and image curation empowered them to make autonomous decisions, improving quality and speed.
    • Onboarding new designers with well-documented processes and templates ensured smoother transitions.
  • AI Integration: Manually performance can be translated to AI prompts once patterns are identified. While AI drastically reduced production time, initial manual fine-tuning was essential to achieve high-quality results. Continuous documentation and prompt refinement improved the AI system over time. AI is costly & it is reliable with the right context and prompt refinement.
