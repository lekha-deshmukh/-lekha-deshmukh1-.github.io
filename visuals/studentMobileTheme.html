Creating the theme for Student Mobile App in LearniCo
The challenge for me was to create a theme which would make students feel that they are in a game.
The theme had to look very inviting, hence I made use of a plethora of colors in the theme. I created 2 versions for college students - dark and light version and also experimented with an option in which students can select pre-built themes on their own for example - Space and Universe, The Jungle book etc.
The dark theme is the one we went ahead with for LearniCo Student Mobile App.
The theme design for student mobile app is highly appreciated by more than 4,000 students. Even during the demo, anyone who used the mobile app once, definitely commented on the unique excellent desing for the dark theme.
