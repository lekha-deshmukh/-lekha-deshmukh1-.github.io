Redefining the in-classroom experience
Goal
I redesigned the teacher experience for conducting classroom using LearniCo software. The goal was to make the in-classroom experience more useful for teachers with LearniCo Teacher mobile app
Context about the project
Teacher conducts the class with the help of presentation that runs in LearniCo offline application. With LearniCo, a teacher can ask different types of questions in the classroom every 5-8 minutes to check students understanding of a topic and build interactivity in the classroom. Students are expected to answer with their smartphones. The entire setup runs offline in the classroom.
Teacher mobile app helps teacher to login in offline environment and sync all online presentations created by teacher at home in offline classroom. The mobile app is also helpful to view which is the previous and next slide in mobile.
Highlights
  • Used by 4,000 students
  • Used by 300 teachers
  • 200 Licenses distributed to colleges across India
RESEARCH
Understanding the problem
The Learning is from Secondary research data about the existing teaching experience in KK Wagh College, Maharashtra
3 out of 5 teachers did not refer the mobile app during the entire session except at times when they posted Question, the mobile app was kept on their desks near the desktop/laptop table at other times.
2 out of 5 teachers used the mobile application during the entire session, to see the next slide and stats related to questions.
The goal was to make the mobile app usable in the entire session

  • Teacher checks understanding of topic in the class by posting questions every 5-8 minutes.
  • Teacher checks the next slide with mobile app
  • Teacher has to walk towards the desktop/laptop to interact with the presentation and change the slide
IDEATION
I did Body-storming as teacher
During body storming as teacher I observed that
  • I stayed near the desktop so that I could change slides quickly, specially when the lecture was coming to an end
  • I was not able to pay attention to all students specially last benchers
  • Seeing the mobile app for checking the next and previous slides felt unwanted once I was near the desktop app
  • I felt that the Students might feel disconnected whenever the teacher was checking the information on mobile app as students didnâ€™t know the purpose behind it
PAIN - Teacher has to walk towards the desktop every time to interact with the application, to change the slide. It disconnects the teacher from the class during this activity.
How Might We (HMWs)
I asked few HMW questions to myself for generating ideas
  • HMW make the mobile app more useful in this entire experience?
  • HMW help teacher be independent of the desktop interaction ?
  • HMW help teacher to move around the class freely?
  • HMW make student feel connected to teacher even when teacher is looking at the mobile?
The most affordable solution I came up with
  • To use mobile phone as a clicker device which will help teacher in navigating through the slides regardless of the place teacher is in the classroom.
  • The mobile app will be synced with the offline application running on the desktop.
  • Mobile app will have previous and next button and on clicking either of them teacher can now show the next slide to entire class.
With the solution of using the teacher mobile app as a clicker device along with its other features, the current experience was elevated
Teacher explains topic to the class
Teacher interacts with students

Teacher changes the slide from mobile app to move on to the next topic & so on
PROTOTYPING
How this idea saved money
Usually clicker devices used for navigating slides costs around Rs. 700 per device, with this idea it saved cost of Rs. 700 per classroom and it can easily save 7K rupees for any small college consisting of 10 classrooms.
FEEDBACK
  • With a 100% success rate, 5 out of 5 teachers started using the mobile application during their classroom sessions
  • Teachers felt that they were controlling the software, than the other way round
  • Contributed as a selling point for the software
